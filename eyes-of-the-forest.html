Eyes of the Forest

A third-person narrative stealth experience where players navigate a dangerous abandoned forest as Thistle, balancing exploration, tension, and environmental storytelling.

Eyes of the Forest is a third-person narrative-driven experience where you play as a field mouse, navigating and stealthing through a long-abandoned forest. Secure a mysterious artifact with your mentor while avoiding the horrific hunters that stalk the woods.

Eyes of the Forest is currently in development. I serve primarily in a level design role with secondary user research responsibilities. Explore preview screenshots and captures from pre-release versions of the game below.
